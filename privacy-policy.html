Privacy Policy

Last updated: Jan 11, 2026

Your privacy is important to me. This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected when you visit this website.

Information collected

This website may collect limited personal information when you voluntarily provide it, such as when you contact me via email or a contact form. This information typically includes your name, email address, and any message you choose to share.

How information is used

Any information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to inquiries or messages
  • Communicate regarding potential collaboration or work opportunities

Your information is never sold, shared, or used for marketing purposes without your consent.

Cookies

This website may use cookies to enhance usability and analyze traffic. Cookies do not contain personally identifiable information. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

Third-party services

Third-party tools (such as analytics providers) may be used to understand how the website is used. These services may collect anonymized data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect your personal information. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have shared. To do so, please contact me directly.
