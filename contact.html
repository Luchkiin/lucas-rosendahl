FAQ

Your questions,
answered

Answers to common questions about my process, services, and how I collaborate with clients.

What kind of clients do you typically work with?

I work with startups, scale-ups, and established companies looking to improve or build digital products. Projects range from early-stage concepts to refining and evolving existing platforms.

What services do you offer?

I focus on product discovery, UX and interface design, and digital production. This includes research and strategy, design and prototyping, and bringing designs into polished, functional digital products.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Smaller projects usually take a few weeks, while larger product or website engagements can span several months. I define clear timelines at the start of each project.

What is your pricing structure?

Projects are typically priced as fixed scopes based on the work required. For ongoing collaborations, I also offer monthly retainers. Pricing is always discussed upfront to ensure clarity on both sides.

Can you work with an existing website or product?

Yes. I often work with existing products — improving usability, refining design systems, or redesigning parts of the experience while respecting what’s already in place.

Do you offer ongoing support after launch?

Yes. I offer ongoing support for iteration, improvements, and maintenance to ensure the product continues to evolve after launch.
